## Common Examples

- US: "AAPL", "GOOGL", "MSFT"
- Swedish: "SHB-A.ST" (Handelsbanken), "VOLV-B.ST" (Volvo)
- UK: "LLOY.L" (Lloyds), "HSBA.L" (HSBC)
- Australian: "BHP.AX", "CBA.AX", "TLS.AX"
- Canadian: "RY.TO" (RBC), "TD.TO" (TD Bank)
- German: "BMW.DE", "SAP.DE"
